An Analysis of Robert Frost's "Birches"

The following paper is representative of some of my work as an English minor. It contains a brief analysis of Robert Frost's "Birches" conducted for an Interpreting Literature Course. It focuses on the imagery of the birch trees, the contrasts of the black and white coloration of the trunk, the tendency of the trees to sway back and forth, as well as their larger implications about life itself.
