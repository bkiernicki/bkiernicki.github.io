Eusociality of Sweat Bees: An Evolutionary Approach

This file contains a powerpoint summary of a scienitfic article entitled "Kinship, parental manipulation and the evoutionary origin of eusociality" (Kapheim et al 2015). It was submitted as a group project for an Animal Behavior course taken at Temple University. The subject in this article is the tropical sweat bee, Megalopta genalis. The main goal of the research within the article was to establish whether eusociality evolved via the daughter's genes (i.e. her willingness to help) or as a result of the mother's genes (maternal manipulation).
