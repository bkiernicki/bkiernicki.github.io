Dementia Care Website

Dementia Care is a simulation website created specifically for use by dementia patients and their families. It was part of an individual project for a Texts and Cultures of Science course taken at Temple University. The intention was to extract information from sites used by medical professionals and translate it into laymans terms with a more empathetic approach intended for a broader audience (in particular the patients and family of the patients) without compromising the effectiveness and accuracy of the content.
